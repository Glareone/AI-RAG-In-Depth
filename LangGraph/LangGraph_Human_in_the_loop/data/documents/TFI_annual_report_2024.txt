NOTE 4 — DEFERRED REVENUE AND REMAINING PERFORMANCE OBLIGATIONS

Deferred revenue represents amounts billed to customers for which revenue has not yet
been recognized. As of December 31, 2024, total deferred revenue was $236,300 thousand.
Remaining performance obligations (RPO) totaled $1,842,600 thousand, of which
approximately 58% is expected to be recognized over the next 24 months. RPO grew 31%
year-over-year, indicating strong forward revenue visibility.

Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 118% for FY2024 (FY2023: 114%).
Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR): $762,400 thousand as of December 31, 2024.

NOTE 6 — DEBT

In March 2022, the Company issued $300,000 thousand of 2.375% senior unsecured notes
due March 2029. The notes are investment grade rated (BBB- / Baa3). The Company
maintains a $200,000 thousand revolving credit facility (undrawn as of December 31, 2024).
