NOTE 5 — DEBT

As of December 31, 2024, total debt was $1,727,000 thousand consisting of:
  - $500,000 senior secured term loan A (due 2027, SOFR + 2.25%)
  - $750,000 senior unsecured notes (5.875% fixed, due 2029)
  - $292,000 revolving credit facility drawn ($400,000 total capacity, due 2026)
  - $185,000 current portion of long-term debt

The Company's credit agreement contains covenants including:
  - Net leverage ratio not to exceed 5.0x EBITDA
  - Interest coverage ratio of at least 2.5x
  As of December 31, 2024, the Company was in compliance with all covenants.
  Net leverage ratio: 4.6x (2023: 3.2x). Interest coverage: 2.8x (2023: 5.8x).

NOTE 8 — RESTRUCTURING

In Q3 2024 the Company initiated a restructuring program (Project Streamline) targeting
$65M in annual cost savings by closing two manufacturing facilities in Ohio and
consolidating European operations. $41.2M in charges were recorded in FY2024.
An additional $18–22M in charges are expected in H1 2025.

NOTE 12 — GOING CONCERN ASSESSMENT

Management has evaluated conditions and events that could raise substantial doubt about
the Company's ability to continue as a going concern. The $185M current portion of
long-term debt due in 2025, combined with the decline in operating cash flow ($201M in
2024 vs. $398M in 2023), requires the Company to refinance or repay this obligation.
Management believes existing credit facilities and projected operating cash flows are
sufficient to meet obligations for at least 12 months, though no assurances can be given.

NOTE 15 — SUBSEQUENT EVENTS

On January 14, 2025, the Company announced the divestiture of its non-core Specialty
Coatings division for $87M in cash, which will be used to reduce revolving credit
facility borrowings.
